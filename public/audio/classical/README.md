# Classical Music Audio Files

These audio files are used for the Classical Music Quiz educational game.

## Source

Audio files sourced from the [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/) collection:
- **Collection:** [100 Classical Music Masterpieces](https://archive.org/details/100ClassicalMusicMasterpieces)
- **Archive.org Collection Type:** `opensource_audio`

## Copyright Status

### Musical Compositions
All musical compositions in this collection are in the **public domain**. The composers (Beethoven, Mozart, Bach, Tchaikovsky, etc.) died more than 70 years ago, placing their works firmly in the public domain worldwide.

### Sound Recordings
The recordings were obtained from Internet Archive's `opensource_audio` collection, which hosts freely distributable content. However, the specific copyright status of individual recordings may vary:

- Some recordings may be public domain due to age (pre-1929 recordings)
- Some may be released under Creative Commons or similar licenses
- The original performers and recording engineers are not always documented

**For commercial use:** We recommend verifying the licensing of each recording or obtaining recordings from sources with explicit public domain dedication, such as:
- [Musopen](https://musopen.org/) - Commissions and releases classical recordings under CC0/public domain
- [IMSLP](https://imslp.org/) - Has a recordings section with clearly labeled licenses
- [Free Music Archive](https://freemusicarchive.org/) - CC-licensed classical recordings
