Match identical Mahjong tiles by connecting them with a path of at most 2 turns!

## A.K.A.

Also known as: Four Rivers, Shisen, Sho

## Category

Spatial & Tile Puzzles

## How to Play

1. Match pairs of identical Mahjong tiles by clicking them
2. Tiles can only be connected if there's a valid path between them
3. The path must have at most 2 turns (90-degree bends)
4. The path cannot pass through other tiles (but can go around the board edges)
5. Clear all tiles from the board to win

## Tips & Strategy

- Look for easily matchable pairs first
- Pay attention to tiles on the edges - they often have simpler connection paths
- The path can extend beyond the board boundaries
- Use the Hint button if you get stuck
