## Category

## How to Play

1. Each tile is divided into 4 triangular sections with numbers 0-9
2. Drag or click tiles from the tray to place them on the board
3. Adjacent tiles must have matching numbers on their touching edges
4. Fill the entire board with all tiles correctly placed
5. Win when all edges match perfectly

## Tips & Strategy

- Start with corner tiles - they have fewer neighbors to match
- Look for unique number combinations that limit placement options
- Color-coding helps identify which numbers appear on which tiles
- Work from edges inward for a systematic approach
