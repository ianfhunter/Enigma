## A.K.A.

Also known as: Tri Peaks, Three Peaks Solitaire

## Category

Card Games

## How to Play

1. Cards are arranged in **three peaks**, each with 4 rows (30 cards total) with a draw pile (22 cards)
2. Only **exposed** cards can be selected (face-up and not covered by cards below)
3. **Draw a card** from the deck - it goes face-up on the waste pile
4. Remove cards from peaks that are **adjacent** (one rank higher or lower) to the top waste card
5. Removed cards go on top of the waste pile and become the new matching target
6. **Win** by clearing all 30 cards from the three peaks

## Tips & Strategy

- The top card of the waste pile is always your matching target
- Look for chains: removing one card may reveal cards that match the new waste card
- Plan ahead: sometimes it's better to draw a new card before removing exposed cards
- Clear peaks strategically - clearing one peak completely can help access cards in others

## Controls

- **Click** a card from a peak to remove it (if adjacent to waste card)
- **Click** draw pile to reveal next card and add to waste pile
- **Undo** to reverse your last move
- **New Game** generates a fresh random puzzle
- **Give Up** to end the current game
